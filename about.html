About Us

Welcome to Spruston Road Camp! We are dedicated to providing a transformative summer experience for children and youth, centered around faith, friendship, and fun. Nestled in the scenic beauty of Spruston, our camp offers a safe and welcoming environment where campers can grow spiritually, build lasting friendships, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

OUR MISSION

At Spruston Road Camp, our mission is to provide a nurturing and inclusive community where campers can deepen their relationship with God, discover their unique talents and gifts, and develop the character and leadership skills needed to thrive in today's world. We believe in the power of Christian education and outdoor adventure to inspire and empower young people to live lives of purpose, integrity, and compassion.

OUR VALUES

  • Faith: We are committed to sharing the love of God with every camper and helping them grow in their faith journey.
  • Community: We foster a supportive and inclusive community where campers feel valued, accepted, and supported.
  • Adventure: We believe in the transformative power of outdoor adventure to challenge, inspire, and empower young people.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty, integrity, and ethical behavior in all that we do.
  • Service: We instill a spirit of service and stewardship in our campers, encouraging them to make a positive difference in the world.
