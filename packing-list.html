What to bring

Wondering what you'll need to bring to camp? Here are some important things that you will need and won't want to forget at home:

PACKING CHECKLIST

  • Bible (if you have one)
  • Notebook + pen
  • Sleeping bag
  • Pillow
  • Water bottle
  • Towel
  • Flashlight
  • Sunscreen lotion
  • Medication (if needed)
  • Clothes for a week of camp, rain or shine
    • Pants
    • Shorts
    • T-shirts/tank tops
    • Socks
    • Underwear
    • Pyjamas
    • Good running shoes
    • Rain jacket
    • Sandals
    • Sweater/hoodie
  • Toiletries
    • Soap
    • Shampoo
    • Toothbrush
    • SocToothpasteks

Cigarettes, alcohol and illicit drugs are prohibited at camp.
